Simple Lists

The simplest way to render a list is with an Item View. It might seem strange to use an Item View to render a list, as opposed to a Collection or Composite View, but there are times when you don't need the added functionality that those views give you.

When To Use

You should use Item Views to render your lists when you don't need to track events, like hovers or clicks, on individual items in the list. When you need to track events you should look into one of those other view classes.
